Agua Fresca

Description

Quench your thirst during these hot summer months with a easy & simple Agua Fresca recipe. Stay Frosty!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 pounds strawberries,hulled and diced,divided
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 (14 1/2 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1(12 ounce) can evaporate milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla
  • 1/2 cup sugar,or as needed
  • 3 cups ice cubes,or as needed

Steps

  1. Place 2 pounds of the strawberries and water in a blender cup and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour strawberry puree into a large pitcher. Add sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla. Stir until well incoporated. Taste;add sugar, a little at a time,as needed.
  3. Add ice cubes and stir. Gently stir in remaining 1/2 pound diced strawberries.Chill until ready to serve.
